Privacy Policy – AutoQuiet

Last updated: May 23, 2025

AutoQuiet ("the App") is developed by bustn. This privacy policy explains how the App handles data.

Data Collection

AutoQuiet does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The App does not require registration or login.

Accessibility Service

AutoQuiet uses Android's Accessibility Service solely to detect advertisements in the YouTube app in order to automatically lower the device volume. No screen content is recorded, transmitted, or stored.

Notification Listener

AutoQuiet uses the Notification Listener permission solely to detect advertisements in Spotify and SoundCloud based on media notifications. No notification content is stored or transmitted.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
